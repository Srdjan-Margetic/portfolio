This is my story

My first contact with programming was in 2013. At that moment, I was still building my carrier as a sommelier on cruise ships.

As I moved up the corporate ladder and became a Cellar Master, I received additional safety duties for my station and my team. I proved that I can learn fast and that I'm a highly responsible person who can take care of my coworkers. Also, with that part, I showed excellence in taking care of more aspects taking care of the needs of my clients as well as taking care of the needs of my employer.

But something was missing.

Whenever I had time, I spent it learning more about programming. At the end of 2018. I made a tough financial decision. I left the cruise ships with my savings and focused all my attention on programming. For me, being a programmer is life stile itself, not only a job and not something that one can do part-time.

That is a time when I took a Front End Development course at the Comtrade academy. Parallel to that, I was watching different tutorials at Udemy and Pluralsight. One year later, I realized that this is only the beginning.

Finally, I started as a Junior Front End Developer at Stuntcoders company, where I had the opportunity to get acquainted with different work environments. I learn to work in diverse team settings and touch many technologies that will open my eyes to further career advancement.

Although I'm still a beginner in this area, I believe that my passion and dedication, as well as my understanding of duties, can help your company grow.
